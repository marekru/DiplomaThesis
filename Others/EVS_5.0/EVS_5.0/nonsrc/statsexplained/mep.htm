The MEPD is similar to the quantile-quantile (Q-Q) plot (Wilks, 2006) and the probability-probability (P-P) plot (Shorack and Wellner, 1986; Gneiting et al., 2007). The Q-Q plot compares the order statistics of two samples, or the order statistics of one sample against the values of a theoretical distribution at corresponding quantiles (Wilks, 2006). The P-P plot compares the quantiles corresponding to these order statistics.
